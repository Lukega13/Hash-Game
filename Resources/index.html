Intructions of the Game

2 players play alternately

Each play places a symbol in each square of the board

First one places "X"s and the Second one places "O"s

The player who first manages to form a line with 3 identical symbols wins, whether horizontally, vertically or diagonally.

If all squares are filled and nobody wins, the game is a draw
